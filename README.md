# Bootcamp_EDA_STUDY
This case study aims to give an idea of applying EDA in a real business scenario. In this case study, we will develop a basic understanding of risk analytics in banking and financial services and understand how data is used to minimize the risk of losing money while lending to customers.

Our primary approach to the EDA of the loan datasets was graphing many different variables against mean values for ‘TARGET’ in order to determine the most predictive variables for default rate – or those which showed the greatest disparity in ‘TARGET’ values across the categories of the variable. 

We also merged the two datasets by ID, allowing us to compare current loan data against historical loan performance and application history, which too proved to be important in determining the creditworthiness of the individuals in the current dataset.
